Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Information We Store

StudyTool stores account details such as email address, display name, role, quiz sessions, answers, scores, and question-bank content. Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to sign you in, show your dashboard, calculate quiz history and stats, manage student accounts, and support administrator workflows.

Administrator Access

Administrators can view student profiles, quiz history, quiz statistics, and account roles. Administrators can also delete student accounts.

Third-Party Services

StudyTool uses Supabase for authentication and data storage and Netlify for hosting and serverless functions. If Google sign-in is enabled, Google may process authentication-related information according to Google's policies.

Data Deletion

Student accounts and related profile or quiz records may be deleted by an administrator. Some operational logs may remain with third-party infrastructure providers for a limited period.

Security

StudyTool uses role-based database policies and server-side functions for sensitive administrative actions, but no system can be guaranteed perfectly secure.
